# Free game directories to submit to

All free. Most take 2–5 minutes per submission. Submit on Day 1 of launch.

| Site | What it is | URL | Approval time |
|------|------------|-----|---------------|
| **iogames.space** | The single biggest io game directory. ~100k MAU. | https://iogames.space/submit | 1–7 days |
| **iogames.land** | Aggregator + curation. | https://iogames.land/submit | 1–7 days |
| **iogamesnow.com** | Reddit-style directory. | https://iogamesnow.com (contact via footer) | 1–14 days |
| **CrazyGames** | Bigger audience. Higher bar — they want polish. Best for after a few weeks of iteration. | https://developer.crazygames.com/ | 7–30 days |
| **Poki** | Curated, kid-friendly bias. Apply only if you've got polish. | https://developers.poki.com/ | 7–30 days |
| **GameDistribution** | Free, fast approval, reasonable traffic. Requires their SDK if you want their ads, but you can submit without. | https://gamedistribution.com/registration | 1–3 days |
| **Kongregate** | Legacy but real. Free upload. | https://www.kongregate.com/games/new | manual review |
| **GameJolt** | Indie-friendly. Free hosting + community. | https://gamejolt.com/games/new | instant |
| **itch.io** | Free upload, no approval. Strong indie crowd. **Do this first.** | https://itch.io/game/new | instant |
| **Newgrounds** | Old guard, niche but loyal audience. | https://www.newgrounds.com/portal/submit | 1–7 days |
| **Y8** | Mainstream casual portal. | https://www.y8.com/contact | 7–30 days |
| **Friv** | Mostly kids, but huge. | https://www.friv.com/submit (or contact via footer) | manual |
| **Armor Games** | Curated. | https://armorgames.com/develop | 7–30 days |

## What you submit (have these ready)

- **URL:** https://playorbital.up.railway.app/
- **Title:** Orbital
- **Short description (140 chars):**
  > Free browser tank shooter. Destroy shapes, level up, dominate the arena. WASD + mouse. No download.
- **Long description (1–3 paragraphs):**
  > Orbital is a real-time multiplayer tank arena io game. Pilot a tank, destroy yellow squares, red triangles, and blue pentagons to earn XP. Each level grants stat points you spend on 8 stats — health regen, max HP, bullet damage, reload speed, and more.
  >
  > Battle other players for the leaderboard. Auto-fire and auto-spin let you grind hands-free. Free to play with no download — works on desktop and mobile. Cosmetic skins and a VIP pass let you support development.
- **Controls:** WASD or arrow keys to move · Mouse to aim · Left click or Space to fire · 1–8 to upgrade · E auto-fire · C auto-spin
- **Tags:** io, multiplayer, tank, arena, shooter, free, browser, diep
- **Screenshots:** 1280x720 PNG. Take 3–4 of: lobby, gameplay, big pentagon kill, leaderboard, death screen.
- **Banner / cover art:** Use `public/og-image.svg` (export as 1200x630 PNG via [cloudconvert.com/svg-to-png](https://cloudconvert.com/svg-to-png)).
