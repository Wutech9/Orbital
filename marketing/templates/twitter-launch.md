## Who to @ (sparingly!)

Don't mass-tag. But if you genuinely want a particular dev to play:
- @itchio (only if you've uploaded to itch.io)
- @CrazyGames (only after submitting via developer portal)
- @diep_io (parody account, low chance of response)
- Other io game devs you actually engage with
