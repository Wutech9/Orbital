How to play

  • WASD or arrow keys to move your tank
  • Mouse to aim — left-click or Space to fire
  • Destroy squares · triangles · pentagons for XP
  • Spend stat points (keys 1–8) to upgrade Reload, Damage, HP, etc.
  • E auto-fire · C auto-spin · F fullscreen
